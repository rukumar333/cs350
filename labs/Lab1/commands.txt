cp
Copies files/folders to a specified location.
Ex. cp file1.txt file2.txt -> Copies file1.text to a new file file2.txt

grep
Searches for text in an input.
Ex. grep "main" fork_proc.c

fg
Switches to jobs running in the foreground

gedit mprog.c &
Runs gedit on mprog.c in the background.

ps
Displays processes, their statuses, and PIDS.

top
Displays running processes, their statuses, PIDS, users, along with other information. Provides a dynamic view of running processes with memory usage as well. 

tar -xvf file.tar
Extracts contents from file.tar. "Untars" file.tar
